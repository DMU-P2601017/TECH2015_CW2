ABOUT

Bubble Bee was founded just recently, in the year 2015, and since then we expanded very quickly around the Europe and Northen America.

We specialize in creating bath bombs which consist of very unique scents like Bubblegum Dream and Vanilla Ice Cream. For all lovers of sweet and fruity fragrances, that will remind you of childhood memories. We are here for you to experience dreamlike bath you deserve.

Lots of our collections are handmade by small communities from Indonesia, Philippines and New Zealand and we want to support them for their love and effort they put in their amazing work!
